There are 3 options for this section of the Greenway

  • Overpass
  • Underpass
  • At Grade
Overpass

This option proposes an overpass that will allow the Greenway to proceed over Vestal Parkway.

Pros:

  • Separates trail users from motor vechicle traffic
  • Highly Visible
  • Identifies trail location
  • Enhances safety
  • No affect on motor vechicle traffic

Cons:

  • Grade may be difficult for some trail users
  • Significantly more expensive to build than any other alternative
  • The most cost-effective prefabricated bridge options are not the most aesthetic
  • Expensive to maintain over the long run
Overall Estimate: $2.75 million
Underpass

This option proposes an underpass that will allow the Greenway to pass under Vestal Parkway.

Pros:

  • Trail is closer to the river than other alternatives
  • Trail is buffered from traffic State Route 434
  • Aesthetics of site adjacent to river
  • It will not affect traffic on State Route 434

Cons:

  • Involvement with State Route 434 embankment
  • Grade on the south approach may be difficult for some trail users
  • Lower visibility than other alternatives
  • Security and vandalism concerns
  • Trail will be "hidden" from State Route 434
  • Could incur maintanence costs not associated with other issues
Overall Estimate: $1.1 million
At Grade

This option proposes an at grade road crossing for the Greenway to cross State Route 434

Pros:

  • Lowest construction and maintenance cost
  • No added security concerns
  • No steep approach or descent

Cons:

  • Least safe for trail users
  • It will affect traffic on State Route 434
  • Doesn't completely separate trail users from motor vechicles creating a conflict point
  • State Route 434 is a high speed facility
  • Snow removal on median crossover may be a problem
Overall Estimate: $0.8 million
